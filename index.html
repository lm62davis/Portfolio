About Me

I've always been curious about how things function. When I was 12 years old, I dismantled a transitor radio to see it's inner workings. I had to know how a box could deliver the sounds of distant people, music, and ideas.

My first job was as a data transcriber at the local Social Security office. My desk was next to the server, which was about the size of a walk-in freezer. Ocassionally it malfunctioned and the service tech would come out. I constantly quizzed him on the cause of the malfunction. We became good friends and he taught me simple hardware fixes.

Although my initial career path wasn't in technology, I always kept coming back to it. I maintained a small foothold by helping friends, family, and even a small business with their hardware and software issues.

Over the years I discovered that my true love was in software. Just change a little code and get a whole new look or functionality to your project. So I focused my attention on front-end applications and web design. As a people-person who embraces technology, the fusion of programming and creativity really spoke to me. So first I attained the Certified Internet Web Masters Web Design Certification. Most recently I graduated from the We Can Code It! Software Developer Bootcamp.

Some projects showcasing my abilities are shown here. Allow me to demonstrate my newfound skillset on your programming needs.
