Franklin Castle embodies so many humanly traits - deep, dark, seductive, mysterious, and misunderstood.  And as edifices go, it's not responsible for such a reputation.  Fortunately change is near.  Renovation has begun to convert the secret passageways, hidden rooms, and even the ballroom into a private dinner club.  How appropriate for a mansion that generates so much discussion to play host to members seeking good food and good conversation. I can just imagine a smoking lounge with big stuffy chairs.  Or a library lined with cherry wood shelves, stocked with classic literature.  Or maybe a sitting room for quiet mediation about the unknown.  (They must be in the blueprints somewhere.)

Seriously, those interested in becoming a member of the  Franklin Castle Club  should visit the official website. Besides exquisite dining in a luxurious setting, accommodations will be available for overnight guests.
